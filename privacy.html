Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Welcome to Live Echo, a live singing app that allows you to sing with your friends and family in real-time. Just connect your speaker via AUX cable or Bluetooth, and experience live voice effects similar to a real physical machine. Your feedback means a lot to us—feel free to share your thoughts on the Play Store.

Permissions and Data Usage

  • Record Audio – Required for capturing your live singing voice.
  • Write External Storage – Used to save your recorded audio files.
  • Read External Storage – Used to access and manage saved recordings.

No Data Collection

We value your privacy. Live Echo does not collect, store, or share any personal data from your device.

Third-party Services

Live Echo does not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising services, or tracking services.
