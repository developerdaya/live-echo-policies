About Live Echo

Turn your phone into a real-time karaoke mic with studio-level voice effects and powerful echo controls! Whether you're a casual singer, a content creator, or a party enthusiast — Live Echo gives your voice a professional, fun, and dynamic sound in real time!

🔥 Key Features

  • 🎧 Live Audio – Hear your voice instantly with no delay
  • 🎙️ Record Audio – Save your performance in real-time
  • 🎚️ Voice Effects – Rich echo effects with customizable controls
  • 🎵 Perfect for Karaoke – Sing with background tracks
  • 🔊 Live Sound Output – Real-time audio feedback like on stage
  • 📱 Plug & Play – Works with headphones or external speakers

🎛️ Custom Echo Controls

  • 🟦 Dry – 100% = pure voice; 0% = only echo
  • 🟪 Wet – 100% = only echo; 0% = echo off
  • ⚡ BPM – Adjust how fast the echo repeats
  • ⏱️ Echo – Set delay between original voice and echo
  • 🌫️ Decay – Control how long the echo lasts before fading

🔌 Connection Options

  • 🎵 AUX Cable – Best experience with zero latency
  • 🎧 Type-C to AUX Converter – For phones without 3.5mm jack
  • 🟦 Bluetooth – Convenient but may cause slight delay

🧑‍🎤 Who’s It For?

Karaoke fans 🎶 | Singers 🎤 | Creators 📹 | Party lovers 🎉
